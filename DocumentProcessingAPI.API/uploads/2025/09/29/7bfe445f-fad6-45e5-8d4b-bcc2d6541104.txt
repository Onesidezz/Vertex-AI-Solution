## Business Segments

### Cloud Computing Division (Revenue: $45.2B, 52% of total)
Our cloud computing division provides Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions to enterprise customers worldwide. Key products include:
- TechCloud Platform: Our flagship cloud infrastructure service
- DataStream Analytics: Real-time data processing and analytics
- SecureVault: Enterprise-grade cloud storage solutions

### Artificial Intelligence Division (Revenue: $28.7B, 33% of total)
The AI division focuses on machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision technologies. Primary offerings include:
- AIAssist Enterprise: Business intelligence and automation platform
- VisionPro: Computer vision API for developers
- LanguageCore: Natural language processing toolkit

### Enterprise Software Division (Revenue: $13.1B, 15% of total)
Traditional enterprise software solutions including:
- WorkFlow Pro: Project management and collaboration tools
- FinanceMax: Enterprise resource planning software
- CustomerLink: Customer relationship management platform
